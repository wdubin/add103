Ornaments

The ornaments are the most important part of a christmas tree. You can hang them up anywhere on the tree wherever you want. The ornaments are shiny and glittery, some of them can be in different colors, like red, blue, or green. Most of the ornaments can be round and sphere, some others can be oval, diamond, or even the shape of a top or the shape of an egg. The first decorated trees were adorned with apples, candy canes and pastries in the shapes of stars, hearts and flowers. But in 1605, a fur tree was brought inside and adorned with paper roses, candles, nuts, and fruits. The most common materials for modern Christmas ornaments are glass, metal, plastic and wood. Ornaments come in a nearly infinite number of shapes, but some of the most common ones are ball ornaments. Other materials used to make Christmas ornaments include holly leaves, holly twigs, pine cones, hickory nuts, and acorns. Glass ornaments were mouth-blown into nuts and fruit molds made of clay.
